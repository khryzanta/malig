Frequently Asked Questions

How should I care for the dress?

We recommend hand washing in cold water or machine washing on a gentle cycle. Do not bleach. Hang or lay flat to dry to preserve the shape and fabric quality.

What material is the dress made of?

This dress is made from a high-quality blend of 95% polyester and 5% spandex for comfort, durability, and a flattering fit.

Is the dress lined or see-through?

The dress is fully lined and designed to be non-see-through. For lighter colors, wearing nude undergarments is still recommended.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders typically ship within 1/2 business days. Delivery time depends on your location, but standard shipping usually takes 3/7 business days.

Can I return or exchange the dress?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided the dress is unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Please see our return policy for more details.
